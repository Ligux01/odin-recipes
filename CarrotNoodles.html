Carrot Noodles

Description

This light and healthy carrot noodle dish features spiralized carrots sautéed with garlic, olive oil, and your favorite seasonings. It’s a low-carb, colorful alternative to pasta—perfect as a quick side or a fresh veggie-packed main.

Ingredients

  • 3 large carrots, spiralized
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tsp lemon juice (optional)
  • Fresh parsley or herbs, for garnish
  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add the spiralized carrots and cook for 3–5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until tender but still slightly crisp.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and a splash of lemon juice if using.
  5. Remove from heat, garnish with fresh herbs, and serve immediately.
