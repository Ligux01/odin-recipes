Avocado Salad

Description

This refreshing avocado salad combines creamy avocado with crisp cucumbers, juicy tomatoes, red onion, and a zesty lemon or lime dressing. It's light, healthy, and perfect as a side dish or a quick, nutritious snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp fresh lemon or lime juice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional: chopped fresh cilantro or parsley
  1. Prepare the veggies: Dice the avocados, cucumber, and halve the cherry tomatoes. Thinly slice the red onion.
  2. Combine in a bowl: Gently mix all chopped ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Make the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together lemon/lime juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Toss and serve: Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat. Garnish with fresh herbs if desired.
  5. Serve immediately for the best texture and flavor.
